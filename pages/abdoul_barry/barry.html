What I Worked On

I worked on a game that would let you bet on the Ether price which was actually interestingly unstable lately. In order to make the game DareCoin depend on the blockchain, I had to write a smart contract in solidity. Well, the whole purpose of a smart contract is to allow a transaction without a trusted third party. It is like an automated protocol that goes active once certains requirements are verified. However, what makes the blockchain so interesting and reliable is because once the smart contract is deployed, there is no way to temper with it or change anything.
